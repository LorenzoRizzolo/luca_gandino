About Luca Gandino

Luca has enjoyed a successful international career at prestigious companies like FIAT, Philip Morris, JLL, and Swedish Match. He's also a published author with a book titled "Business Black Belt," highlighting his dedication to merging martial arts and business.


In addition to his corporate achievements, Luca has ventured into war zones in Syria to support educational projects, leaving his mark in twelve countries across all continents throughout his 30+ year career. Starting as an Italian Muay Thai champion, Luca excels in various martial arts like Kung Fu, Kickboxing, Full Contact, and even won the 2018 and 2022 Abu Dhabi Pro in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.


Recognizing how martial arts principles influenced his success, Luca pioneered a groundbreaking approach in 2016, fusing martial arts and business. His innovative method, born from profound insights, emphasizes discipline, focus, resilience, and teamwork, which he successfully applies to corporate success and shares with others.


Luca's BBB method is a potent fusion of business and martial arts principles, equipping participants with the mindset and skills to excel in any field. It's a pioneering approach to personal and professional growth, empowering individuals to achieve their goals and lead their best lives.
